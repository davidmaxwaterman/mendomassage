Benefits of Mendocino Massage on the Coast

Our goal is to profoundly improve your physical well-being, enhance relaxation and refine your state of mind leaving you with a greater sense of peace and general wellness.

The Physical Benefits of Therapeutic Massage

Massage relieves muscle tension and stiffness and can bring about faster healing of strained muscles and sprained ligaments. Pain, swelling and muscle spasms can be reduced. Greater joint flexibility and range of motion can result. Massage promotes deeper and easier breathing and improves the circulation of blood and movement of lymph fluids. Blood pressure may be reduced and tension-related headaches relieved. It can enhance athletic performance and skin health. Regular massage will improve posture and strengthen the immune system. During pregnancy, any discomfort is often relieved by prenatal massage.

Mental Benefits of Therapeutic Massage

Massage will enhance your peace of mind promoting a relaxed state of alertness. Mental stress will be reduced allowing an increased capacity for calm thinking and creativity . Massage fosters a feeling of well-being , reduces anxiety and increases the awareness of your  mind-body connection. In short, you will feel wonderful after your massage, ready to fully embrace whatever your day brings.
