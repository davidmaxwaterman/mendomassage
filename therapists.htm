Our Massage Therapists

We are a group of six practitioners who live in the small village of Elk, fifteen beautiful miles south of Mendocino, right on the coast.

We are all experienced massage therapists and fully certified. Beyond our qualifications though, we are committed to tailoring your treatment to your particular massage preferences and fully meeting your needs. We resolve to improve your overall sense of well-being, address specific problem areas and encourage rejuvenation.

We often work together and if you prefer couples massage can bring two tables and work along side each other.
