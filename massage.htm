Massage Treatment Menu

Our therapists each have their own signature style which will be a combination of various techniques. Please discuss with your practitioner the level of pressure that brings your body the greatest benefit and any allergies you may have.

Swedish Massage

The Swedish style of massage offers long flowing strokes, kneading, cross fiber manipulation and some gentle rocking . Oil, lotion or body cream will be used.

Deep Tissue Massage.

Deep Tissue massage is designed to offer relief for tension in the muscles that underlay the superficial layer and will involve a deeper and more focused pressure. Oil, lotion or body cream will be used.

Pregnancy Massage.

Massage is a perfect way to reduce stress and promote well-being during pregnancy. The mother will be made comfortable with the support of numerous pillows and asked to be in a side lying position. We use only non-scented lotions and oils to respect the sensitivity that often occurs in pregnancy. Please advise your practitioner as to your stage of pregnancy and any particular issues you wish to alleviate.

In all our treatments you will be treated in a professional manner. We will leave the room while you disrobe to your comfort level and get onto the table between the massage sheets, before we return. You will remain covered with the sheets and a warm blanket during the massage. Only that part of your body being worked on will be uncovered.

Please remove all jewelry.

We can bring music if you like or if you have your own will be happy to play that.
It is a good idea to remain well hydrated as one of the great benefits of massage is detoxification. Please drink plenty of water.
