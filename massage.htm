Massage Treatment Menu

Our therapists each have their own signature style which will be a combination of various techniques. Please discuss with your practitioner the level of pressure that brings your body the greatest benefit and any allergies you may have.

Swedish Massage

The Swedish style of massage offers long flowing strokes, kneading, cross fiber manipulation and some gentle rocking . Oil, lotion or body cream will be used.

Deep Tissue Massage.

Deep Tissue massage is designed to offer relief for tension in the muscles that underlay the superficial layer and will involve a deeper and more focused pressure. Oil, lotion or body cream will be used.

Healing Stone Massage.

Healing stone massage uses water-heated stones to apply pressure and heat to the body. Warm stones lightly coated in oil are used by the therapist to deliver various massaging strokes. The stones used are locally sourced river rocks which over time, have become extremely polished and smooth. As the stones are placed along the recipient's back, they help to retain heat which then deeply penetrates into the muscles, releasing tension. Please allow at least an hour advanced booking for this treatment to prepare the rocks.

Acupressure Massage.

Acupressure is a traditional Chinese medicine technique derived from acupuncture. With acupressure physical pressure is applied to acupuncture points by the hand or elbow to relieve specific muscle tension, physical and emotional maladies. Several of our practitioners are experienced in Jin Shin acupressure a modality where combinations of acupressure points are held simultaneously to bring about relief to particular concerns.

Reiki Massage.

Reiki Massage originated in Japan. In this technique the practitioner will transfer healing energy through the palms. We are fortunate to have in Elk a certified Reiki master, Peggy Phillips. She is an independent attuned to various lineages. Her style is a synthesis of Reiki techniques and other hands on/over modalities that she has learned since beginning her career as an energy/body worker.

Pregnancy Massage.

Massage is a perfect way to reduce stress and promote well-being during pregnancy. The mother will be made comfortable with the support of numerous pillows and asked to be in a side lying position. We use only non-scented lotions and oils to respect the sensitivity that often occurs in pregnancy. Please advise your practitioner as to your stage of pregnancy and any particular issues you wish to alleviate.

You will remain covered with the sheets and a warm blanket during the massage. Only that part of your body being worked on will be uncovered.

Please remove all jewelry.
