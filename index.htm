Welcome to Mendocino Massage on the Coast

Whether you are visiting Mendocino and the beautiful Mendocino Coast for a few days, or live here full-time, we would love to help you feel relaxed, rebalanced and refreshed. With a genuine desire to meet your individual needs, we can offer a fabulous massage in your choice of location.

Our Mendocino massage studio is conveniently located right in the village of Mendocino at 45180 Main Street directly across from the bay. We can also meet you in the Elk studio next door to Queenie's restaurant or, should you prefer, we are happy to come to your home, hotel room or vacation house.

Each of our practitioners is an experienced, skillful and fully certified therapist who will listen and respond to your particular treatment preferences to best ensure you enjoy and fully benefit from your massage.

Sessions are either 75 or 90 minutes long and can be given in tandem for couples. We offer a deep tissue option, healing stones, Jin Shin Acupressure, Reiki and Prenatal massage.
